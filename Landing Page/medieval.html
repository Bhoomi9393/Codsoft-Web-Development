Villains of the Medieval Era

  • A fallen priest using forbidden magic to control minds and rewrite fate.

  • An armored ghost who executes both tyrants and heroes indiscriminately.

  • Ruler of a cursed valley, whose vines consume entire armies.

  • A trickster who brings chaos with laughter, illusions, and traps.

Player Characteristics

  • Balanced Combat: Use a mix of sword, bow, and shield tactics.
  • Alchemy & Forging: Craft potions, poison, or enchanted weapons.
  • Honor System: Choices affect your reputation with kingdoms and factions.
  • Beast Training: Tame gryphons, direwolves, or even dragons.
  • Siege Skills: Build catapults and defend or raid castles.
