Quests

It consists countless dynamic quests but here are some major ones listed:

Recover the lost Relics

Find legendary artifacts scattered across forbidden jungles and deserts

Awaken the Ancients

Perform rituals to communicate with ancient spirits guarding the truth

Complete challenges left by a mysterious civilization that mastered time long before you.

Villains of Ancient Era

  • Corrupted sorcerers who once worshipped the gods but now drain power from the land.

  • Undead guardians cursed to protect sacred tombs.

  • Giant serpents, elemental golems, and mythical creatures that test your strength.

  • A warlord cursed for defying the gods; he controls an undead army.

  • A shape-shifting sorceress who tests your moral choices.

Player Skills

  • Increased Instinct: High perception for uncovering hidden paths and secrets.
  • Limited Technology: Rely on raw survival skills, not machinery.
  • Spiritual Sensitivity: Ability to interact with spirits, visions, and time echoes.
  • Resourceful Crafting: Use bones, stone, wood, and fire to build unique items.
  • Weapons & Tools: Bone knives, obsidian swords, ritual staffs.
  • Taming pets: Taming wild beasts to fight alongside you and harmless ones to get resources and services.
